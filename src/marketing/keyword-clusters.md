## Usage tips

1. **One primary keyword per page** in title and first paragraph.  
2. **Secondary keywords** in H2s, body, and image alt text.  
3. **Long-tail** in blog posts and guide titles.  
4. **Avoid stuffing;** keep copy natural and useful.  
5. **Refresh** this doc when adding new pages or campaigns.
